What is an account?

An account is a logical grouping of transactions. It may be a literal bank account, or it might be something be something more abstract, like a grouping of your assets or liabilities (debt).

Account Types

There are four account types. They are:

  • General: Used for day to day transactions.
  • Investment: Used for tracking your investments.
  • Asset: Used for tracking things that you own which hold significant value.
  • Liability: Used for tracking your debts.

Most people would have one of each account type.
