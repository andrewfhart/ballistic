Ballistic Finance is an online finance tracker. It can track general accounts, investments, assets, and debts. It can also predict your retirement age based on your goals and investment history. It is primarily a motivational tool to help people improve their financial health. It was created by Jeremy Miller and you can find the source code on GitHub.

For more information, check out the announcement post.
