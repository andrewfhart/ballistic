Welcome to Ballistic Finance

Before you get started, you should know a bit about the philosophy behind ballistic.

Ballistic is not about budgeting or tracking your transactions with absolute accuracy. Its purpose is only to inform you of your financial health and cause you to reflect more often on it.

With this in mind, you are encouraged not to take it too seriously. The only requirement when working with your accounts is that their balances match your real balances at the end of every month. If you want to write out every credit card transaction you make in a month, that's fine, but it's also fine to just lump them together and enter the total.

If you get too concerned about absolute accuracy, then you are less likely to continue tracking your transactions. This is much worse than tracking them poorly. As long as you are thinking about your financials at least monthly, you are doing well.

Financial Freedom

Ballistic is built around the idea of reaching financial freedom. Financial freedom is the point when your investments earn enough to replace your income. This means you are not longer required to work to live.

Financial freedom is different from most retirement plans in that there is no calculated date where you will not longer need the income (i.e. at death). Your investments will earn you income indefinitely, assuming the economy remains in good health. This means that not only will your future be secure, but potentially the future of your descendants as well. Most of the super rich are super rich due to having ancestors who saved instead of spent.
